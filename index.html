Frequent questions

How can I contact you and quickly get a quote for my online project?

The best way to reach us is through the contact form or by messaging us on our social media accounts. For a fast quote make sure you provide many project details.

Do you create designs from the ground up or you are using themes?

We can work both ways. For clients with specific needs and budget, We create custom designs from scratch. For clients looking for more affordable solutions, We can customize existing themes to match their brand.

Will I receive any included maintenance or warranty after project delivery?

Yes, all our projects come with 30 days of free maintenance for any issues that might appear. After this period, we can discuss a maintenance plan if needed.

If something goes wrong with the project can I have my money back?

We take pride in our work and will do everything to make sure you're satisfied.

What's your preferred method of payment and do you need an advance?

We typically require a 50% advance before starting the project. The remaining payment is due upon completion. We accept bank transfers, PayPal, and other digital payment methods.
